# Salmon with Honey Mustard Glaze

## Ingredients

- 1 lb salmon fillet

### Honey Mustard Marinade

- 1 tsp brown sugar
- 1 tsp melted butter
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 1 clove garlic, minced

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, mix all marinade ingredients.
2. Place the salmon , skin side down, in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over it. Cover and refrigerate for at least 15 minutes but no longer than 1 hour.
3. Set oven control to broil. Remove salmon from marinade and place skin side down on a broiler pan.
4. Broil salmon for about 10 to 15 minutes, or until the fish flakes easily with a fork.
5. Remove from oven and let rest for a few minutes before serving.
