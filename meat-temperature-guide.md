# Meat Temperature Guide

## Pork

- **Pork Shoulder/Chops:** 145°F.
- **Pork Ribs:** 195°F - Tender and easily pulls apart.

## Beef

- **Steak (Medium-Rare):** 130-135°F - Warm red center.
- **Steak (Medium):** 140-145°F - Warm pink center.
- **Steak (Medium-Well):** 150-155°F - Slightly pink center.
- **Steak (Well-Done):** 160°F - Little to no pink.
